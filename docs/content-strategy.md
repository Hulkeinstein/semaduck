# Directive: Content Strategy & Brand Identity (Sema Duck Farm)

This directive serves as the **Single Source of Truth** for all content-related decisions, copywriting, and design adaptations.

## 1. Brand Identity: Sema Duck Farm (세마오리농원)

### Core Facts
- **Establishment**: Since 1998 (26+ years of heritage).
- **Location**: Osan, Gyeonggi-do (Secluded, nature-friendly location).
- **Specialty**: Fresh Sulfur Duck (유황생오리) grilled over Charcoal (참숯).
- **Philosophy**: "Farm to Table" freshness (No frozen meat), Rustic Comfort.

### Menu Data (Actual Content)
| Category | Menu Item | Description/Characteristics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Main** | **생오리 한마리 (Fresh Duck Whole)** | Signature dish. Roasted over high-quality charcoal. Served with coarse salt. Savory, non-greasy. |
| **Stew** | **오리탕 (Spicy Duck Stew)** | Served after the roast. Deep, spicy broth made with duck bones. |
| **Sides** | **영양죽 (Porridge)** | Savory finish. Made with duck stock and grains. |
| **Sides** | **도토리묵 (Acorn Jelly)** | Popular appetizer/side. rustic seasoning. |
| **Sides** | **냉면 (Cold Noodles)** | Refreshing finish. |

## 2. Benchmark Reference: Diodona Ristorante

**URL**: `https://www.diodona.it/ristorante/`
**Concept**: Elegant Rustic, Farmhouse Luxury.

### Structural Mapping Strategy

We adapt Diodona's Italian farm content to Sema's Korean duck farm context.

| Section | Diodona Original Content | Sema Duck Adaptation Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Intro** | "Sapori di casa" (Flavors of home) | **Heritage of Taste (Since 1998)** <br> Focus on the timeline and the owner's stubbornness for quality. |
| **2. Chef** | "Il nostro Chef" (Our Chef) | **Master of Fire (The Artisan)** <br> Focus on the *process* of handling charcoal and grilling, rather than a specific celebrity chef. |
| **3. Sourcing** | "Filiere autentiche" (Authentic chains) | **Fresh & Local (Ingredients)** <br> Highlight: "Daily slaughtered fresh duck" & "Local seasonal vegetables". |
| **4. Menu** | "Menu" & "Specials" | **Signature Courses** <br> Presenting the standard flow: Roast -> Stew -> Porridge as a "Course". |
| **5. Space** | "Gli ambienti" (Environments) | **Rustic Retreat** <br> Emphasize the "Garden", "Family Gathering", and "Healing" vibe. |
